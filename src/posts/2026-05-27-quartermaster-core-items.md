Quartermaster Core. The base content overlay for Quartermaster. ~60 items across three categories, to flavor your loot caches - no tier required.

**Treasure (36 items).** Art objects and trade goods to add character and flavor, not just coinage to the loot caches.

**Trinkets (10 items).** Pocket curiosities. No real gold value, but they help 'flavor' the caches

![](https://c10.patreonusercontent.com/4/patreon-media/p/post/159373428/4e81500175dc4438875da9091a1e0700/eyJhIjoxLCJ3Ijo4MjB9/1.png?token-hash=t9kCFs7wJjGdKiLXE0C9nD2gzzqOzKc81eyuO1cr2uE%3D&token-time=1783641600)

**Containers (15 items).** Terrain-tagged chests, crates, and cache objects for the cache generator. Forest, desert, swamp, urban, and dungeon each get their own set.

![](https://c10.patreonusercontent.com/4/patreon-media/p/post/159373428/3aad7ecad616436ba4ef0afcb361a125/eyJhIjoxLCJ3Ijo4MjB9/1.png?token-hash=MXkimWfMF4psYDvFnnofj1bDttwkfBMiHuFgNcp0i80%3D&token-time=1783641600)

* * *

Terrain-specific packs are coming for subscribers. The core overlay is the foundation; the terrain layers are what make each find feel like it belongs somewhere.

_**Automatic:**_ _Ionrift Library → Quartermaster → Core pack → Install__**Manual:**_ _[https://api.ionrift.cloud/packs/public/quartermaster-core-overlay/latest](https://api.ionrift.cloud/packs/public/quartermaster-core-overlay/latest)_ _→ Install .zip in the Library_
